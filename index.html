Dennis Macalister Ritchie

Dennis MacAlistair Ritchie was an American computer scientist. His nickname is ‘Dmr’. He was born in1941 in Bronxville, New York. He studied physics and applied mathematics in Harvard. He began to work the Bell Labs in 1967. He and Ken Thompson (his colleague) created C Programing Language for created UNIX operating system in the Bell Labs. Nowadays, C programing language is still using. It is a foundation of lots of programme languages like, C++, C#, Java. His most famous sentence is “UNIX is simple operating system but you should clever to understand the operating system.” He has written ‘ UNÝX Programmer’s Manuel’ in 1971 and ‘ The C Programming Language’ in 1978. He gets Turing Awards in 1983 and International Technology Medal in 1999. He died on October 12, 2011, at the age of 70.
